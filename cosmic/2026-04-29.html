Narasimha Jayanti: The day the Lord appeared as man-lion from the pillar to protect His devotee Prahlāda. The avatāra appeared at twilight (neither day nor night), from the pillar (neither inside nor outside), and placed Hiraṇyakaśipu on His lap (neither earth nor sky). Perfect synthesis of opposites.

Hasta → Chitrā: The hand yields to the brilliant gem. Knowledge becomes radiant when received with devotion. What was hidden becomes visible.
