TITHI Amāvāsyā (New Moon)
NAKṢATRA Śatabhiṣā (from 10:46 AM)
YOGA Śiva (from 01:58 PM)
VĀRA Maṅgalavāra (Tuesday)
RĀŚI Kumbha Rāśi (Moon)
RĀHU KĀLA 02:49 PM - 04:08 PM

COSMIC ALIGNMENT:

Amāvāsyā (New Moon) brings the darkest night, yet Śiva Yoga activates during this darkness. The apparent emptiness creates space for pure Śiva consciousness to emerge. The Moon moves from Śravaṇa (listening) to Śatabhiṣā (healing/detoxification) at 10:46 AM - a shift toward purification and release.

VERSE ALIGNMENT:

BG 2.66 speaks of the undisturbed mind - perfect for the dark clarity of Amāvāsyā. When external sight is limited, internal vision becomes acute. The one who remains connected (yukta) during the darkness finds the śānti that eludes the distracted.
