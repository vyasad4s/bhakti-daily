 >>> COSMIC INTERPRETATION <<<

Amāvāsyā — The dark moon, the conjunction point where Sun and Moon merge. Total absence of reflected light — the prakṛti at its most obscuring. Perfect for the verse on disconnection (ayuktasya).

Śatabhiṣā — "The Hundred Healers," "The Hundred Physicians." Symbolized by an empty circle or medicine/physician's circle. After Dhaniṣṭhā's drum comes the healing. But the verse warns: healing requires connection (yukti) — the physicians cannot treat a patient who refuses to show up.

Śiva Yoga — Named for the destroyer/transformer who sits in meditation. Activates at 01:58 PM. The yoga of dissolution and renewal — but also, the yoga of the supreme yogi who is always yukta (connected).

 >>> VERSE SELECTION RATIONALE <<<

nāsti buddhir ayuktasya
na cāyuktasya bhāvanā

BG 2.66 — Yesterday established prasāda. Today: the inverse — what happens without yukti. The ayuktasya lacks the prerequisites for happiness: buddhi → bhāvanā → śānti. The cascade runs backwards. The Hundred Healers cannot operate on a system that lacks the foundational yukti.
