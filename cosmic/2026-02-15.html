 >>> COSMIC INTERPRETATION <<<

Caturdaśī — the 14th day, the eve before Amāvāsyā. Mahā Śivarātri: the Great Night of Śiva. The night when darkness peaks and the conditions for deepest tapas are perfect.

Śravaṇa — "The Listening." The ear-shaped constellation, presided over by the Moon. The nakṣatra of hearing — fitting for a verse about receiving prasāda. Where Vyatipāta scattered, Śravaṇa gathers — sound into meaning, vibration into wisdom.

Variāṇa yoga — "The Excellent One." After Vyatipāta's dissolution, excellence and virtue activate. The transition occurs at 04:16 PM.
