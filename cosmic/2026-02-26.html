Saptamī tithi — the seventh day, the number of completion in the material world. Seven chakras, seven notes, seven forces. The multiplicity that ultimately points to the One.

Ārdrā nakṣatra — "the Howler," Rahu's constellation. The storm god, the tearful one, intense effort and purification through suffering. This nakṣatra of transformation gives way before noon to...

Punarvasu nakṣatra — "Return to the Good," Aditi's star. Wealth, prosperity, revival, shelter. The storm gives way to the rainbow. This is the cosmic mirror of BG 3.3: storm transforms into shelter; effort transforms into grace.

Vidaal Yoga — special, prominent, the divider. This yoga marks things as significant—cosmic emphasis on the TWO paths being revealed as ONE truth.

Guruvāra — Jupiter's day, Bṛhaspativāra. The day of wisdom, teaching, and divine guidance. The answer to Arjuna's question comes on the day of the guru.
