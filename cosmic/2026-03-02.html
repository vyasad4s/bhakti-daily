🔥 Cosmic Interpretation

Chaturdaśī — the day before fullness. The eve of Pūrṇimā in Śukla Pakṣa. The karma-yogī stands at the threshold of illumination.

Magha — the throne, royal seat. Deity: Pitṛs (forefathers). The superior yogī must embody Magha energy — legitimate authority through integrity, not pretended.

Pūrva Phālguni — the marriage bed, divine union. As Moon enters this nakṣatra at 09:01 PM, energy shifts from rulership to relationship. The karma-yogī realizes devotion is union with the Supreme.

Sukarma → Dhṛti Yoga — from good action to steadiness. The mark of the superior practitioner is persistence, not perfection.

Somavāra (Monday) is the Moon's day — Chandra day of reflection, transformation, and the purified mind.
