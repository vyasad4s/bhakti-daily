 >>> COSMIC INTERPRETATION <<<

Today carries the energy of annihilation and renewal. Papamochani Ekādaśī is not merely about fasting—it is about the complete destruction of accumulated duḥkha. The tithi itself is named for this function: pāpa (sin) + mocana (liberation). The karmic ledger gets wiped clean.

Śravaṇa nakṣatra enters at 07:19 PM—this is the asterism of listening, of śravaṇa in its root sense. Presided over by Viṣṇu and symbolized by three footprints, Śravaṇa is the nakṣatra of divine sound and sacred transmission. Combined with Ekādaśī, this is a day for receiving teaching, not merely performing austerity.

Parigha yoga (iron club/bolt) suggests obstacles being struck down. What seems like a harsh energy is actually protective—it destroys that which would obstruct the spiritual path. The iron club clears debris.

Meena Saṅkrānti at 03:30 PM marks the sun's entry into Pisces—the final month of the Vedic year. This is dissolution energy—old structures dissolving before renewal begins. Combined with Papamochani Ekādaśī, today is a complete reset.
