> SYSTEM: Aṣāḍhā nakṣatras preside over the unconquered waters (Āpaḥ).

> ANALYSIS: Water transforms; water cannot be destroyed.

 

[ VERSE SELECTION RATIONALE ]

BG 3.9 reveals the alchemy of sacrifice — how ordinary karma becomes yajña. Pūrva Aṣāḍhā's "unconquered" quality requires this teaching: work performed for Viṣṇu cannot be bound, just as water cannot be destroyed. The Śiva yoga ensures transformation of reaction into liberation. This is not theory — Arjuna stood on the battlefield when Kṛṣṇa spoke these words.
