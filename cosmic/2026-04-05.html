>>> COSMIC INTERPRETATION <<<

Sankashti Chaturthi — The fourth day of the waning moon dedicated to Lord Gaṇeśa, remover of obstacles. This tithi asks: what blocks your action? What internal resistance prevents the work that must be done?

 

Viśākhā → Anurādhā — The moon moves from the "forked branch" (duality, scattered energy) into "subsequent success" (devotion, aligned will). This is the cosmic signature for today's teaching: move from conflicted action to consecrated action.

 

Siddhi Yoga — The yoga of accomplishment, success, attainment. Not the ego's achievement, but the fruition that comes when action is yoked to purpose beyond the personal.
