Rohinī nakṣatra — the star of growth, the red one, the beloved. Prajāpati presides over this nakṣatra of fertility and abundance. Rohinī means "thereddish one" — the cow that gives abundant milk, the earth that yields crops, the womb that nurtures life.

Vaidhṛti Yoga — propitious, favorable, free-flowing. The constraints are temporary; the flow is natural. This yoga supports the transition from renunciation to engaged action.

Maṅgalavāra — Mars-day, Tuesday. The warrior's energy applied to ordinary life. Karma Yoga demands exactly this: martial discipline applied to householder life.
