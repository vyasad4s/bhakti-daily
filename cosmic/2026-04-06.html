The transition to Jyeṣṭhā brings Indra's energy — leadership, protection, authority — but through Gandamool's restructuring fire.

 

Vyatīpāta yoga warns: the path is obscured. What appears righteous may be twisted. This is not a day for major beginnings or new contracts after 5:26 PM. It is a day for executing existing duty with extra care for precision and detachment.

 

Kṛṣṇa Pañcamī is Pañcamī of dissolution — the fifth step in the march toward Amāvāsyā. The month is Caitra (Amānta) or Vaiśākha (Pūrṇimānta) — the threshold between solar years, between endings and beginnings. The energy favors completion, discharge of obligation, disciplined maintenance of dharma despite uncomfortable conditions.

 

The Moon enters Vṛścika (Scorpio) — its sign of debilitation — before Amāvāsyā. Emotional intensity, but turned toward depth rather than display. The bhakti here is not celebration but endurance — showing up, doing the work, releasing the fruits.
