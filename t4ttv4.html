>>> FOUNDATION :: THE TWO ETERNALS
00 | PURUṢA (पुरुष) The Witness, Pure Consciousness Nirvikāra (unchanging), Sākṣī (witness), Asaṅga (unattached) Not an evolute - distinct from all 24 Reference: BG 13.19 - "prakṛtiṃ puruṣaṃ caiva"
01 | PRAKṚTI (प्रकृति) :: PRADHĀNA (प्रधान) The Primordial Matrix, Mūla-Prakṛti (root matter) Jaḍa (inert), Pariṇāmi (transforming), Triguṇātmikā (three-guṇa) Before manifestation - unmanifest (avyakta) Reference: SK 3 - prakṛteḥ mahāṃś ca
>>> CHURNING :: THE THREE GUṆAS
02.A | SATTVA (सत्त्व) Lucidity, Harmony, Wisdom From root AS (to be) - luminosity Buddhi-tattva emerges from sattva-predominance Reference: BG 14.6 - "sattvaṃ sukhe sañjayati" 02.B | RAJAS (रजस्) Activity, Passion, Motion From root RAJ (to color/redden) - energetic endeavor Fuels manifestation, drives evolution forward Reference: BG 14.7 - "rajo rāgātmakaṃ viddhi" 02.C | TAMAS (तमस्) Inertia, Darkness, Resistance From root TAM (to become exhausted) - heaviness Coagulant that holds form, obstructs clarity Reference: BG 14.8 - "tamas tv ajñānajaṃ viddhi"
>>> EVOLUTION :: THE 24 TATTVAS
03 | MAHAT (महत्) :: BUDDHI (बुद्धि) First Evolute - The Cosmic Intellect Functions: Judgment, discrimination, will, virtue Predominantly sattvic - reflects puruṣa when pure Reference: SK 22 - buddhir adhyavasāyaḥ; YS 1.2
04 | AHAṂKĀRA (अहंकार) I-maker, Self-identity, individuation From AHAṂ (I) + KĀRA (doing) Creates subject-object split, limits awareness Three branches: Sāttvika, Rājasa, Tāmasa Reference: SK 24 - abhimāna ātmānam abhimanyate
05-09 | JÑĀNENDRIYAS (5 Sense Organs - Sāttvika Branch) 05 | ŚROTRA - Ear/Hearing 06 | TVAK - Skin/Touch 07 | CAKṢUS - Eye/Sight 08 | RASANĀ - Tongue/Taste 09 | GHRĀṆA - Nose/Smell Reference: SK 26-27 - for perception of sound, touch, form, taste, odor
10-14 | KARMENDRIYAS (5 Action Organs - Sāttvika Branch) 10 | VĀK - Speech/Mouth 11 | PĀṆI - Hands 12 | PĀDA - Feet 13 | PĀYU - Excretion 14 | UPASTHA - Procreation Reference: SK 28-29 - for expression, giving, moving, excreting, enjoying
15 | MANAS (मनस्) Internal Coordinator, Eleventh Organ From Sāttvika ahaṅkāra but connecting to all Functions: Saṅkalpa (intention), Vikalpa (doubt), citta-vṛtti Reference: SK 27 - saṅkalpakam manaḥ
16-20 | TANMĀTRAS (5 Subtle Elements - Tāmasa Branch) Called "those only" (tad-mātra) - the essences 16 | ŚABDA-TANMĀTRA - Sound-essence 17 | SPARŚA-TANMĀTRA - Touch-essence 18 | RŪPA-TANMĀTRA - Form-essence 19 | RASA-TANMĀTRA - Taste-essence 20 | GANDHA-TANMĀTRA - Smell-essence Reference: SK 38 - tanmātrāṇi viśeṣāṇām yonayaḥ
21-24 | MAHĀBHŪTAS (5 Gross Elements - from Tanmātras) 21 | ĀKĀŚA - Ether/Space (from Śabda-tanmātra) 22 | VĀYU - Air (from Sparśa-tanmātra) 23 | TEJAS - Fire (from Rūpa-tanmātra) 24 | ĀPAS - Water (from Rasa-tanmātra) 25 | PṚTHVĪ - Earth (from Gandha-tanmātra) Reference: SK 38; BG 7.4 - "bhūmir āpo 'nalo vāyuḥ"
>>> REGULATORS :: KARMA & KĀLA
KARMA (कर्म) :: The Algorithm Law of Action-Reaction (kṛṣṇi - to do) Governs all prakṛtic manifestation Not punishment/reward - mechanism of balance Reference: BG 3.19 - karmaṇy evādhikāras te KĀLA (काल) :: Time The Irreversible Sequence Makes transformation possible Destroyer and Creator (Mahākāla) Reference: BG 10.30 - kālaḥ kalayatām aham
