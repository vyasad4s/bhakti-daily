Cosmic Context for May 10, 2026

  • Nakṣatra: Shatabhisha (May 10 03:20 PM – May 11 03:58 PM) - Known as "the hundred healers," associated with healing, meditation, and mysterious energies
  • Yoga: Indra (May 10 04:38 PM – May 11 03:34 PM) - Associated with Indra (king of gods), representing power, leadership, and divine authority
  • Vāra: Raviwar (Sunday) - Ruled by Sun, representing vitality, leadership, and divine energy

  • Surya Rāśi: Mesha (Aries) - Initiation, courage, pioneering spirit

Cosmic Theme

Today's energies converge on divine leadership in action: The Navami tithi brings completion, Shatabhisha nakshatra adds healing purity, Indra yoga provides divine authority, and Sunday's solar energy fuels righteous action. This creates a powerful conduit for performing work in devotion with sense control—exactly as described in BG 5.7.

Yoga Sutra Connection

The Yoga Sutras' Kriya Yoga (II.1) provides the perfect framework for BG 5.7: tapas (discipline) aligns with sense control, svādhyāya (self-study) cultivates purity of soul, and Īśvara-praṇidhāna (devotion to God) is the essence of working in devotion. Additionally, Pratyāhāra (II.54-55) - withdrawal of senses - directly supports the verse's emphasis on conquered senses (jitendriyaḥ). When one practices Kriya Yoga with Pratyāhāra, action becomes devotion, and devotion becomes liberation.
