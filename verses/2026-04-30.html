🏹 BG 4.4 | April 30, 2026

 

ज्ञानं तेऽहं सविज्ञेयम् अत्राश्चर्योऽपि विस्तृतः ।

यज्ज्ञात्वा नेह भूयोऽन्यज्ज्ञातव्यं अवशिष्यते ॥ ४ ॥

 

jñānaṁ te 'haṁ sa-vijñeyaṁ atra āścaryo 'pi vistṛtaḥ | yaj jñātvā neha bhūyo 'nyaj jñātavyam avaśiṣyate || 4 ||

 

Śrī Bhagavān said: I shall now declare to you in full this knowledge, the knowable, and its realization — what, knowing which, nothing else here remains to be known.

 

Meta-Knowledge: BG 4.4 reveals knowledge as the object itself. Not information about knowledge, but knowledge realizing its own object. Sa-vijñeyam means knower, known, and knowing become the same continuum.

 

Amāvāsyā Reset: Today's dark moon is the perfect zero-point — like wiping clean the buddhi disk before writing a new OS kernel. The transmission immediately restores functionality without legacy debris.

 

🔥 SĀṄKHYA-FIRST PRINCIPLES

1. Puruṣa ↔ Buddhi Interface: Normally buddhi reflects puruṣa; here buddhi joins reflection. Jñānaṁ sa-vijñeyam cancels any split between reflector and reflected.

 

2. Epistemic Reduction: Collapses anumāna (inference) into āgama (testimony → direct). The Lord speaks — Śabda becomes Pratyakṣa channel.

 

3. Bhāva-Vastu: Jñānaṁ now self-existing reality (bhāva-vastu). This accelerates vidyā-vināśa-vardhan — wisdom eclipses ignorance completely.

 

4. Zero-Fill: Jñātavyam avaśiṣyate — nothing remains unknown. The final nirvikalpa.
