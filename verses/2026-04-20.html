🏹 BG 4.8 | APRIL 20, 2026

 

परित्राणाय साधूनां विनाशाय च दुष्कृताम् ।

धर्मसंस्थानार्थाय सम्भवामि युगे युगे ॥ ८ ॥

 

paritrāṇāya sādhūnāṁ vināśāya ca duṣkṛtām
dharma-saṁsthāpanārthāya sambhavāmi yuge yuge

 

To deliver the pious and to annihilate the miscreants, as well as to reestablish the principles of religion, I Myself appear, millennium after millennium.

 

Three concurrent processes in the divine mission: paritrāṇa (deliver sādhus), vināśa (remove duṣkṛtas), dharmasaṁsthāpana (reestablish cosmic order). Not sequential—these fire simultaneously, like a system restore that cleans malware, restores drivers, and reboots the OS in one operation. The sādhu is defined functionally: Kṛṣṇa consciousness. Not external morality—the guṇas simulate righteousness. The binary check is consciousness.

 

🔥 YS I.23 — īśvara-praṇidhānād vā — "Or [samādhi is attained] by surrender to the Lord." The Gītā reveals WHEN the Lord descends (to deliver devotees); the Yoga Sūtra reveals HOW one becomes eligible: through praṇidhāna—complete surrender. Divine descent and devotee ascent meet at total surrender. Like root access: the system is open, but you need proper credentials. Terrifying for ego—which is the point. Ego must be dismantled for the Lord to be installed.

 

TERM DEFINITIONS:

paritrāṇa — deliverance, rescue; the Lord's primary mission is saving, not destroying

sādhu — the pious; functionally: one in Kṛṣṇa consciousness (the binary check)

vināśa — annihilation; removal of obstruction, not vengeance—deleting malware to restore integrity

duṣkṛta — miscreant; running corrupted gunas-code, unaware of infection

dharmasaṁsthāpana — reestablishing cosmic order; restoring original source code

yuge yuge — millennium after millennium; cyclical time, spiral recurrence

īśvara-praṇidhāna — surrender to the Lord; highest yoga practice, giving root access to the Divine Administrator
