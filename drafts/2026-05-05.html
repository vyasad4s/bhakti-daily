Bhagavad Gita 5.28

यतेन्द्रियमनोबुद्धिर्युक्तो योगवतिष्ठति ।
युञ्जन्नेवात्मनात्मानं पश्यन्नात्मनि चात्मनः ॥२८॥

Word-for-word: yatendra (restrained senses), manaḥ (mind), buddhiḥ (intelligence), yuktaḥ (engaged), yoga-vatiṣṭhati (established in yoga), yuñjan (meditating), eva (certainly), ātmani (in the self), ātmānam (the self), paśyan (seeing), ātmani (in the self), ātmanāḥ (through self).

Translation (Vedabase.io): One who restrains the senses, mind, and intelligence and engages in devotion while seeing the Supersoul equally present everywhere, becomes immovable in yoga.
