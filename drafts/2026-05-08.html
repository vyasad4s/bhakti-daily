🏹 Bhagavad Gita 5.11

The portal of Parigha Yoga opens at dawn: a cosmic brace that focuses the mind inward, turning even obstacles into fuel for purification.

यत्नाद्यतः क्रियन्तोऽपि कर्माणि नियताः सन्यासिनः । स ञ्ज्योगी समतायुक्तो वासुदेवपरः सुचिः ॥ ११ ॥

yatantaḥ kurvanto 'pi karmāṇi niyatāḥ sannyāsinaḥ | sa yogī samatā-yukto vāsudeva-paraḥ suciḥ || 11 ||

Translation (Vedabase.io): "The yogīs, while acting with great effort, renounce attachment to the fruits of their actions. Such a purified soul, equipoised and fixed solely on Vāsudeva, is freed from all impurities."
