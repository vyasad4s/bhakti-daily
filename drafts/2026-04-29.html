🏹 VERSE CONTENT

Source: BG 4.8

Date: April 29, 2026

Observance: Narasimha Jayanti

Sanskrit:

परित्राणाय साधूनां विनाशाय च दुष्कृताम् ।

धर्मसंस्थानार्थाय सम्भवामि युगे युगे ॥ ८ ॥

Transliteration:

परित्राणाय साधूनाम्
विनाशाय च दुष्कृताम्
धर्मसंस्थानार्थाय
सम्भवामि युगे युगे

Translation:

In order to deliver the pious and to annihilate the miscreants, as well as to reestablish the principles of religion, I advent Myself millennium after millennium.

Commentary:

Kṛṣṇa reveals His threefold mission: **paritrāṇa** (deliverance of the sādhus), **vināśa** (elimination of the duṣkṛtas), and **dharmasaṁsthāpana** (reestablishment of dharma). These are not sequential but simultaneous operations — the Lord descends to accomplish all three at once.

Paritrāṇāya sādhūnām — primary mission: deliverance. The sādhu is not merely moral but fully surrendered. The Lord comes first to rescue His own, like a mother who would run into fire to save her children. This is prema — spontaneous, causeless, all-accomplishing.

Vināśāya ca duṣkṛtām — the secondary operation. Not punishment but purification. Śrīdhara Swāmī comments that the miscreants destroyed are released from their own destructive patterns. The atheist is freed from his atheism.

Dharmasaṁsthāpanārthāya — reestablishment of cosmic order. Dharma is not "religion" as institution but the natural law of consciousness — that the soul is servant, the Lord is supreme. When this truth is obscured, the Lord appears.

Sambhavāmi yuge yuge — cyclical manifestation. The Lord does not appear once but perpetually — whenever the conditions arise. Time is not linear; the Lord's advent is eternal recurrence.

Narasimha Jayanti: Today marks the appearance of Lord Nṛsiṁhadeva, the man-lion avatāra. Hiraṇyakaśipu thought himself invincible — protected by Lord Brahmā's boons. But the Lord manifested from the pillar, neither man nor beast, at twilight (neither day nor night), and placed him upon His own lap (neither earth nor sky). The Lord preserves His devotee Prahlāda and destroys the demon's body while liberating his soul.

Yoga Sūtra Connection:

🔥 YS II.1 — tapaḥ-svādhyāyeśvara-praṇidhānāni kriyā-yogaḥ

"Austerity, study of sacred texts, and surrender to the Lord constitute Kriyā-yoga."

Kriyā-yoga is the practical preparation for meeting the Lord when He descends. The three practices — tapaḥ (discipline), svādhyāya (study), and īśvara-praṇidhāna (surrender) — create the vessel capable of receiving the advent. The Lord's descent is **yugapat** (simultaneous) with the devotee's preparation.

Sanskrit Terms:

  • paritrāṇāya — for deliverance; the primary mission
  • sādhūnām — of the saintly, the surrendered souls
  • vināśāya — for annihilation; elimination of obstacles
  • duṣkṛtām — of the miscreants, those who do wrong
  • dharmasaṁsthāpana — reestablishment of religious principles
  • artha — for the purpose of
  • yuge yuge — millennium after millennium; cyclical time
  • sam-bhavāmi — I manifest, I appear; I become present

🔥 COSMIC CONTEXT

Date: April 29, 2026 | Śukla Pakṣa

Narasimha Jayanti — Caturdaśī Amavatya Eve

Panchāṅga Data:

  • Tithi: Śukla Caturdaśī → Amāvāsyā
  • Nakṣatra: Hasta → Chitrā
  • Yoga: Harṣaṇā → Vajra
  • Karaṇa: Vanija → Viṣṭi
  • Vara: Maṅgalavāra (Tuesday)

Cosmic Interpretation:

Caturdaśī → Amāvāsyā: The darkest night approaches — the threshold where forms dissolve. Narasimha appears at this juncture: neither fully dark (Amāvāsyā) nor light (pratipad), but the exact moment where destruction enables recreation.

Hasta → Chitrā: The hand of action (Hasta) yields to the brilliant gem (Chitrā). Intelligence and discrimination become radiant. The Lord's hand — man-lion claws — cuts through illusion with precision.

Harṣaṇā → Vajra: The yoga of joy/elevation gives way to the diamond thunderbolt. Soft attraction becomes adamantine force. The Lord's mercy becomes His justice; the same hand that caresses Prahlāda annihilates Hiraṇyakaśipu.

System Notes:

  • [NARASIMHA PORTAL] Caturdaśī evening — the threshold advent
  • [AMĀVĀSYĀ APPROACH] Dark moon eve — forms dissolve
  • [VAJRA YOGA] Diamond thunderbolt — invincible force
